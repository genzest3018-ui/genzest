About GENZEST

The Modern Finance & AI Platform Built for Gen Z

We Started GENZEST Because Nobody Was Talking to Us

Most finance websites are built for 45-year-old portfolio managers. The language is dry, the design is boring, and the advice assumes you already have ₹50 lakh sitting around.

We are 18 to 30. We are navigating student loans, first jobs, side hustles, crypto crashes, AI disruptions, and a job market that changes every 6 months. The old playbook doesn't work for us.

GENZEST is the platform we wished existed.

We cover AI, investing, crypto, startups, and the future of work — in plain English, without the jargon, without the condescension. Just real information that helps you make smarter decisions with your money, your career, and your future.

GENZEST was built independently by two young founders passionate about the future of AI, finance, investing, and digital media. What started as an idea between two creators quickly became a platform focused on helping the next generation stay informed, financially smarter, and future-ready.
